Salmon Fillet

Description

Salmon fillet paired with asparagus creates a delightful and nutritious dish that is both visually appealing and flavorful.

The salmon fillet is typically seasoned with a blend of herbs and spices, enhancing its rich, buttery flavor. When cooked, it becomes flaky and tender, often highlighted by a squeeze of fresh lemon juice that adds brightness.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • Â½ teaspoon salt
  • 4 (6 ounce) fillets salmon
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 lemon wedges

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Stir garlic powder, basil, and salt together in a small bowl.
  3. Rub the mixture evenly over salmon.
  4. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Add salmon and cook until browned and flaky, about 5 minutes per side.
  5. Serve salmon with lemon wedges.
